Creaturetopia

Alice B Hutley has lived her life in the rural area of Wapengo New South Wales. 40 minutes from the towns of Bermagui and Bega, she has spent much of her free time exploring nearby bush land.

From a young age Alice had a fascination with books. She especially loved escaping into the alternate worlds in various fantasy novels and series. Later she found a similar enjoyment creating her own.

As digital education rose, she developed an interest in the interactive web based entertainment. She found herself drawn to pet simulation games, so much so that she has a strong desire to work in that industry. She has been teaching herself programming skills since grade nine highschool and working on a text-based pet simulation game of her own in pursuit of this.

Alice B Hutley lives with her parents. She spends much of her free time at home walking with her miniature poodle, Eagle. Other afternoons she spends time in town playing Dungeons & Dragons with various groups.
